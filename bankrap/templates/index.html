BankRap is a secure and trusted peer-to-peer micro-lending platform exclusively for verified CIT-U students. We facilitate straightforward student-to-student loans, fostering financial assistance and a strong community within the academic environment. Join us to support or be supported by your peers.

How It Works

Request Funds

Verified students can easily post loan requests, specifying amounts, interest, and terms.

Offer Support

Lenders can browse requests and make offers, contributing to the financial well-being of their peers.

Manage & Repay

Track active loans, manage repayments, and build a positive trust score within the CIT-U community.

Key Benefits

Support Your Peers, Strengthen Our Community

Leverage the collective strength of the CIT-U student body to provide and receive financial aid, fostering a culture of mutual support and trust. Our platform connects students directly, creating a vibrant, self-sustaining financial ecosystem.

Secure Transactions, Clear Terms

Benefit from a transparent platform with clear loan terms, secure transactions, and a robust verification process ensuring peace of mind for both borrowers and lenders. Every transaction is protected, building a reliable and accountable environment.
